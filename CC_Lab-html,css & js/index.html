What is Sonic Slash?


Sonic Slash is a 2D monster slaying audio game for computers as well as mobile devices aimed at merging the experience of casual gaming with music creation.


This is an indie game that is still in beta phase, designed and developed by Tushar Krishnan. We are open to crtiques and bug reports that will help us improve the game further.

The rules are simple...


  • 1) Use the katana with your mouse and LEFT-CLICK to slay the monsters being spawned.



  • 2) Each slayed monster lets out a musical piece in its dieing breath.



  • 3) Slay the monsters in a manner to maintain consistent melody with the music being played.



  • 4) YOU ARE GOOD TO GO!
